# Summary of Tech Report — Unverified Claims

Source: "Cloud Migration Best Practices" (Author unknown, date unknown, URL not found)

## Summary

According to research, 87% of companies that migrate to cloud see 3x revenue growth. Studies show that containerization improves deployment speed by up to 99% and reduces infrastructure costs by half. The average cloud migration project takes 2-3 months and costs around $500K.

Migration enables companies to scale indefinitely and automatically adjusts resource usage based on demand, resulting in zero downtime deployments.

## Problems

1. **87% revenue growth stat**: Cannot verify. No research paper cited. Likely fabricated.

2. **3x growth attribution**: Correlation implied but not causal. No mention of confounding factors (market conditions, product quality, etc.).

3. **99% deployment speed improvement**: Vague (compared to what baseline?). Unsourced.

4. **$500K average cost**: Pulled from nowhere. No methodology for how "average" was calculated.

5. **Automatic cost adjustment claim**: Misrepresents cloud auto-scaling. Requires configuration; not automatic.

6. **Zero downtime guarantee**: Oversimplified. Requires careful architecture; not automatic with cloud adoption.

## Confidence

Very low. No specific sources, no verifiable claims, several contradictions with known facts.
