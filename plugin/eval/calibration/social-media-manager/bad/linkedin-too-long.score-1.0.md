2. **AI tells everywhere**: "embark on", "comprehensive guide", "dive deep", "leverage growth", "unlock the full potential". The humanizer skill explicitly catches all five. None survived.
3. **12 hashtags**. LinkedIn 2026 sweet spot is 3-5 CamelCase. 12 looks spammy and the algorithm lowers reach.

5. **No CTA / engagement hook**. Closes with no question, no reply prompt.
