# Launch / Feature Announcement Email Template

Use for product launches, major feature releases, integrations, or pricing changes. Goal is a single conversion event: try the new thing.

## Subject line (3 variants)

Test all three on a 10% slice before sending to the full list.

1. **Embargo**: `Tomorrow, 9am PT: {feature_name} ships`
2. **Hype**: `It is here. {feature_name} is live.`
3. **Benefit-led**: `Cut {metric} in half with {feature_name}`

Keep under 55 characters. The benefit variant usually wins for cold lists; the hype variant wins for engaged users.

## Preview text

100-130 characters. Add the proof or scope detail the subject does not have room for.

> Built with feedback from 200 beta users. Available on all paid plans starting today.

## Hero (one sentence)

What shipped, in plain words. No buzzwords.

> {Product} now imports from Notion in two clicks — no API key, no copy-paste.

## Why it matters (one paragraph)

3-5 sentences. Name the old workflow, name the friction, show how the new feature removes it. Quantify if you can.

> Until now, moving content from Notion meant exporting markdown, fixing broken image links, and hand-rolling frontmatter. Customers told us it took an hour per project and they hated every minute of it. The new importer handles images, tables, embeds, and metadata in one pass. Average migration time in the beta was four minutes.

## Show, do not tell

Embed one of:

- 30-second screen recording (Loom, Mux, or hosted GIF, max 5MB for inline)
- Annotated screenshot showing the before/after
- Short product video with captions burned in

Always include alt text for accessibility and dark-mode safe colors.

## Try it now (step-by-step)

Three to five steps. Each step one sentence. Action verbs.

1. Open `{Product} → Settings → Integrations`
2. Click `Connect Notion`
3. Pick the workspace and pages to import
4. Hit `Run import` — done

## Social proof / testimonials

One quote from a beta user. Real name, real role, real company. Avoid generic praise.

> "We migrated 400 docs in twelve minutes. The image links survived. Buy these people lunch."
> — Priya M., Head of Content, Acme

If you do not have a quote yet, swap in a stat: `Beta users imported 47,000 pages in the first week`.

## CTA (primary)

One button. Specific verb. Link goes straight to the feature, not the homepage.

> [Try the Notion importer →]

Backup text link below for clients that block buttons:
`Or paste this in your browser: {url}`

## FAQ (3-5 anticipated questions)

**Will this work on my plan?**
Available on Pro and Team. Free plan can run one test import.

**What about Confluence / Google Docs?**
Confluence ships next month. Google Docs is in design.

**Will it overwrite my existing pages?**
No. The importer creates a new folder by default. You choose where it lands.

**Can I undo an import?**
Yes — every import is reversible from `Settings → Imports → History` for 30 days.

**Does it support private pages?**
Yes, if you grant the Notion integration access to those pages during connect.
