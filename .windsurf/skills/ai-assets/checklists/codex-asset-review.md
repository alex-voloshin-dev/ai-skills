# Windsurf Asset Review Checklist

- The asset uses English only.
- The asset matches Windsurf primitives instead of Claude-only concepts.
- Critical constraints are near the top.
- Policy and reference material are separated.
- References to `.agents/skills` or `AGENTS.md` resolve correctly.
- No machine-specific paths are embedded.
- The asset is concise enough to justify its token cost.
- The asset has a clear purpose and activation boundary.
