# Content Research Workflows

## Topic Research

Before writing:

1. review top-ranking content for the target topic
2. note angle, depth, and freshness
3. identify what competitors miss
4. choose a differentiated angle
5. define primary and secondary keywords plus search intent

## Data and Fact Gathering

Rules:

- prefer primary sources
- avoid stale data unless it is foundational
- keep exact citation details
- verify suspiciously strong claims against multiple sources

## Existing Content Audit

For blog or public content:

- scan the current content library
- rate internal-link relevance
- plan outbound and inbound links
- identify adjacent content gaps for future work

## Blog Content Brief Template

Capture:

- research summary
- audience and knowledge level
- primary and secondary keywords
- outline
- supporting data points
- cross-link plan
- visual requirements
- target word count and reading time
